Hi! Thanks for taking the time to read me. Let me tell you a bit about me and share some thoughts. I'm a passionate Tech professional with over 15 years of experience in several roles, such as Infrastructure Support, Security, Compliance and Auditing.

Throughout these years, and specially after getting into the Internet world, I've came closer to web development, and that's where it all started. By getting deeper and deeper into it, I developed a passion, which made me slowly but steadily learn about the technologies being used. What I find the most interesting about it, is that Internet is one of the main drivers of change in modern life. Society is more and more engaging with web-based solutions that solve real needs: learning new skills, asking for help, online shopping, communication and entertainment are only a few examples of areas in which Technology is changing the rules of the game, and I strongly believe we are wittnessing just the beginning of this revolution, and that's what motivated me to get into this field. So, learning the tools and processes needed to architect solutions that can have a positive impact on people. In other words, democratizing access to information and tools that will help society grow and evolve.

In 2016 I've started my formal training process to become a Web Developer. Throughout these years I've attended web bootcamps, online training, freelance projects and tech communities. All of these experiences have been truly nourishing to understand what the role of a WebDev entails. As of 2021, finds me very actively engaged with a number of side-projects, such as building social networks for workout groups, e-commerce stores and landing pages. I'm also attending technical trainings on Microservices and Microfrontends, and learning new tools, such as Tailwind CSS for styling, Typescript, Docker/Kubernetes for containerization, and setting up of DevOps workflows.

It's been a great journey so far, but I know it's only the beginning. I'm keen to work on interesting projects, and build things together!
